Privacy and Cookies Policy

This document explains how Coparentes Sp. z o.o. processes personal data related to the use of the Coparentes.ai website and how cookies work on the site.

Controller: Coparentes Sp. z o.o., Wigury 26/8, Łódź, Poland · contact: coparentes@gmail.com

1. Data controller and scope of data

The data controller is Coparentes Sp. z o.o., with its registered address at Wigury 26/8, Łódź, Poland. In matters related to personal data processing, you may contact the controller at coparentes@gmail.com.

When you use the website, we may process data voluntarily provided in e-mail correspondence as well as technical data such as IP address, device type, browser, visit date or server logs.

2. Purposes and legal bases of processing

Data is processed to ensure website operation, handle correspondence, provide security, compile statistics and establish, pursue or defend against claims. Depending on the purpose, the legal basis may be the controller’s legitimate interest, pre-contractual measures, a legal obligation or the user’s consent.

3. Recipients and retention period

Data may be entrusted to hosting providers, e-mail providers, IT support, analytics providers or legal advisers only to the extent necessary for a given purpose. Data is stored for as long as needed to handle the matter, ensure website security or until consent is withdrawn where consent is the legal basis.

4. User rights

Data subjects have the right to access their data, rectify it, erase it, restrict processing, transfer data, object to processing and withdraw consent. Users also have the right to lodge a complaint with the President of the Personal Data Protection Office in Poland.

5. Cookies and similar technologies

We use necessary cookies required for the proper operation of the website and for storing privacy preferences. Additional functional, analytics and marketing cookies are activated only after the user gives consent through the cookie banner.

You can change your consent at any time by clicking the “Cookie settings” button in the footer. Restricting cookies in the browser may affect some website functions.
